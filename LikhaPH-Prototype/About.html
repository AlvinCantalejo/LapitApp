THE APPLICATION.

LAPIT is an ordering web application that primarily aims to collect the orders of the buyer, as well as to display the products and services offered by the sellers. The team conceptualize the term LAPIT from a filipino word near, denoting proximity.

It is an avenue for small local businesses to connect to their consumers by providing them the platform and a proximity-based promotion. The team believes that bringing technology down to the hands of the masses is a means of giving profound ways to build capacity and improvement in their own lives.
